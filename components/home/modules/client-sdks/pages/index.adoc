= Client SDKs (Mobile Wallet)

Ost Client SDKs enable developers to integrate a secure, non-custodial layer-2 Ethereum wallet into consumer applications. The SDKs enable end-users to hold & transfer tokens within your application.

The SDKs
. Generate and store keys on the user's mobile device
. Sign Ethereum transactions and data as defined by contracts using EIP-1077
. Enable end-users to recover access to their wallet in case they lose their authorized device
